Introduction

About This Proposal

This proposal outlines a marketing plan designed specifically for Li's Spa. It's built around two goals: growing your Instagram presence to 1,000 followers and driving more booked appointments and repeat business.

We've reviewed your website, services, existing offers, online reviews, and brand positioning to develop a strategy grounded in where you are today and where you want to go.

Two Connected Goals

Strategy

How We Get There

Five interconnected channels — each reinforces the others.

01
Instagram Content & Growth
Full content strategy — calendar, feed posts, Reels, Stories — plus in-spa tactics to convert foot traffic into followers.
02
Local SEO & Google Business
Optimized profile with photos, service listings, weekly posts, and systematic review generation.
03
Reviews & Social Proof
Automated post-visit review requests, testimonial content, and consistent response protocol.
04
Partnerships & Referrals
Outreach to local businesses, healthcare providers, fitness studios, and corporate wellness programs.
05
Retention & Loyalty
Post-visit follow-ups, rebook reminders, birthday offers, and a membership concept for recurring revenue.

Paid Advertising (When Ready)

$300–500/month on Instagram/Facebook targeting Redmond/Eastside residents, introduced once the organic foundation is solid.
