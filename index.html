Strategy

How We Get There

Our approach combines five interconnected channels. Each one reinforces the others — social media drives awareness, local search captures intent, content builds trust, conversion systems turn interest into bookings, and retention systems turn first visits into long-term clients. Your core retainer covers the first three directly; partnership and retention strategy is woven into your monthly strategy sessions and content planning.

01

Instagram Content & Growth

Full content strategy — calendar, feed posts, Reels, Stories — designed to grow your audience and drive bookings. Plus in-spa tactics to convert foot traffic into followers.

02

Local SEO & Google Business

Fully optimized Google Business Profile with photos, service listings, weekly posts, and a systematic review generation strategy to dominate local search.

03

Reviews & Social Proof

Automated post-visit review requests, testimonial content for Instagram, and a consistent response protocol for every review.

04

Partnerships & Referrals

Outreach to local businesses, healthcare providers, fitness studios, and corporate wellness programs for cross-referral partnerships.

05

Retention & Loyalty

Post-visit follow-ups, rebook reminders, birthday offers, and a membership concept that creates predictable recurring revenue.

Paid Advertising (When Ready)

When the organic foundation is solid (typically Month 2–3), we recommend introducing a modest paid ad budget on Instagram/Facebook and potentially Google. Even $500/month can produce strong returns when targeting Redmond/Eastside residents with the right offer.
